I am a researcher scientist in the Autonomous and Smart Systems Unit at Tekniker, located in Eibar, Basque Country. Currently I am carrying out a Ph.D degree on Artificial Intelligence and Robotics.

My research work mainly focuses on applying Artificial Intelligence methods such as deep reinforcement learning (RL) or deep learning (DL), both in computer vision and robotics applications.
